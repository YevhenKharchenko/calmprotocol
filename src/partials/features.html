Features of Aegis Protocol

Bullet Points:

  • Endless Wave Mode: Enemies just keep coming - seriously, they don't stop, and it gets pretty intense.

  • Modular Space Station: Customize your defense by building different types of modules with unique abilities.

  • Persistent Progression: Earn points, upgrade your station, and unlock new ways to defend your core.

  • Clear Arcade Mechanics: Easy-to-understand gameplay with a focus on strategic defense and planning.

  • Stylized Sci-Fi Visuals: It's got that clean sci-fi vibe - none of that gory stuff that'll mess with your sleep.
