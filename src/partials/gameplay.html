Simple to learn, but you'll be scratching your head on the later levels

The concept is simple:

  • Drag and stack colorful hexagonal blocks to solve each puzzle.

  • Match, sort, and arrange the blocks to complete each level.

  • Think ahead and you'll rack up better scores

  • Each level presents unique challenges, with harder combinations and tricky sorting situations as you progress.

Each level gets trickier, so you'll really have to think, but it stays fun throughout There's this great 'click' feeling when you nail a tricky move with those bright hexagon pieces!
