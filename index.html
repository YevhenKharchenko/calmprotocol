Space Defense That Actually Makes Sense

In Aegis Protocol, you have complete control over the defense of your space station:

  • Endless Waves: Fight against waves of increasingly difficult enemies.

  • Modular Station: Build and upgrade various modules to protect your core.

  • Destructible Modules: Every module can be destroyed, so plan your upgrades carefully.

  • Direct Control: Easy-to-learn controls to manage your station, upgrade systems, and make decisions on the fly.

As the waves intensify, your focus on upgrading, maintaining, and protecting your space station will be tested. Can you keep your core stable through the onslaught?
